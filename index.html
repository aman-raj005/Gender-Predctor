Welcome to Baby Gender Predictor

This tool helps predict your baby's gender based on the lunar calendar method. Here's how to use it:

  1. Enter your full name
  2. Input your age at conception (years and months)
  3. Optionally, use the date calculator if you're unsure about your age
  4. Add your address (optional)
  5. Click "Generate Prediction" to see the result
